About Sigma IT Superstore

Established in 1995, and founded with the mission to bring the latest technology to Lucknow, Sigma IT Superstore is a leading retail chain of Brand Exclusive Stores. With around 25 store locations across Lucknow, we pride ourselves on being a one-stop-shop for all your IT needs, offering a vast collection of Laptops, Mobiles, Printers, and Accessories.

Our strength lies in our partnerships with top brands and our commitment to customer satisfaction. Our knowledgeable staff are always on hand to provide expert advice, ensuring you find the perfect product to meet your needs.
